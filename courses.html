Our Driving Courses

DriversLane Driving Academy offers structured driving programs designed to develop knowledge, skills, and confidence on the road.

Theoretical Driving Course (TDC)

The Theoretical Driving Course focuses on traffic laws, road signs, and safe driving practices. This course prepares students for the LTO written examination and builds a strong foundation for practical driving.

  • Traffic rules and regulations
  • Road signs and markings
  • Defensive driving techniques
  • Basic vehicle maintenance
Course Duration Schedule
Theoretical Driving Course 15 Hours Weekdays / Weekends

Practical Driving Course (PDC)

The Practical Driving Course provides hands-on driving experience with professional instructors. Students practice real-road driving techniques to build confidence and driving competence.

  • Vehicle familiarization
  • Road positioning and parking
  • City and highway driving
  • Safety and emergency procedures
Package Sessions Training Vehicle
Beginner Package 5 Sessions Manual / Automatic
Advanced Package 10 Sessions Manual / Automatic
